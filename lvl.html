What are LVL Lashes?

The LVL Lash Lift by Nouveau Lashes is a treatment that combines both an eyelash perm and an eyelash tint treatment. The best thing about it is that it enhances what you already have. It is 100% natural looking and gives you a look thats full of life. With lash lifts, you get the upward curl of extensions, but there are no fake eyelashes in sight. Your eyelashes are pressed around a curled silicone shield, then different bonding formulas are applied. This gives you a semi-permanent lift that adds a similar curl effect to extensions, but using your natural lashes, giving you a more upward, realistic curl that you could try (and fail) to get from using curlers at home.

NOTE: Before booking treatment a patch test needs to be taken 48 hours in advance

Frequently Asked Questions

How does the LVL lash lift work?

Your lashes are covered in a special serum that straightens them from the root, which rather than actually straightening gives them that super curled look. Your lashes are then tinted and, if you’re a little sparse, you might be offered some individual lash extensions as well.

How long does the LVL Lash Lift take?

Typically LVL Lash treatments last between 45 minutes to an hour and most of that time is spent waiting for things to develop– the serum, the dye. Your eyes are shut the entire time, so you might actually find yourself falling asleep. This is not uncommon.

Can you still wear mascara with an LVL Lash Lift?

Although an LVL treatment includes a lash tint which helps to create the appearance of longer and thicker lashes without the need for mascara, it’s still completely safe to wear should you wish to enhance the results further. However, we recommend following our aftercare advice and waiting until 48 hours after your treatment.

Can you get an LVL treatment wet?

For the first 48 hours after your treatment, avoid getting your eye area wet. This is because your eyelashes can take up to 48 hours to lock into place and if they get hot or wet, the lift will drop. After this time period, there are no restrictions with an LVL lash lift, so you can continue to shower, swim and use saunas without protecting your eye area.

Is there an age limit to have an LVL Lash Lift?

Anyone from the age of 18 can have an LVL lash lift. This treatment is perfect for mature eyes as it can instantly open and refresh the eye area, so you appear bright eyed and ready to go.

Lash lift with tint vs. eyelash extensions

Deciding whether to have extensions or a lash lift with tint is all a matter of how thick and dark you want your lashes to appear. If you have fairly full and dark lashes naturally, a lash lift with tint would give you a natural curled lash look with added impact. If you're looking for some definition without the commitment of extensions, these are the perfect solution as your lashes will be appear more curled, fanned out and darker.
